Education & Continuous Education

Traditional education (A.K.A. a University degree) is not anymore the only way to get a fancy job in the corporate world and, depending on the country or job market you are located at, it isn't even a safe guarantee of getting any job at all (not to mention a good job). New skills are in demand, and those who move fast to get the right skills will have more chances of succeding at their roles.
You can gain the skills you want by doing a Master's degree, or by taking a bunch of online courses too (this option is getting more and more popular). Regarding Data Science and ML, I have taken (or am still taking) the following ones, which I'd recommend:
