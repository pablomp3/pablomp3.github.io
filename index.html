What I do

I am currently living in Taipei and working as a Data Scientist at Titansoft, a software company from Singapore.

During my studies I have been quite active; leading Machine Learning research for music composition and also participating in several side-projects.

These are some of the projects I feel more proud about:

Variational Autoencoders for Polyphonic Music Interpolation

Research paper based on my Master Thesis, where I proposed a new Machine Learning method for music composition that outperforms the state-of-the-art. Published at the 25th International Conference on Technologies and Applications of Artificial Intelligence (TAAI 2020).

MooDoor: An Interactive Door with Emotion Display to Strengthen Social Capital and Community Resilience

I co-authored a paper that was accepted to the 31st Australian Conference on Human-Computer-Interaction (OzCHI'19).

Sound Processor for Electric Guitar

My Bachelor in Electronic Engineering Thesis consisted on the hardware development of a programmable digital effects pedal for electric guitar, based on Digital Signal Processing technology, and integrating the new hardware together with the software from a previous project.

Credit card payment

This mini project focus on credit card payment prediction, and lead to landing an interview at my current employer.
